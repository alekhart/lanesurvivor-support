Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Introduction

Lane Survivor ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile game.

Information We Collect

Lane Survivor is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Gameplay Data: Your game progress, scores, achievements, and settings are stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device.
  • Purchase Information: In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect or store any payment information.

Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address)
  • Location data
  • Contact information
  • Device identifiers for tracking purposes
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data

Third-Party Services

Apple App Store: If you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is handled by Apple. Apple's privacy policy governs how they handle your purchase information. We only receive confirmation that a purchase was made, not your payment details.

Data Storage

All game data (progress, settings, statistics) is stored locally on your device using standard iOS storage mechanisms. This data is included in your iCloud backup if you have iCloud backup enabled for your device. We do not have access to this data.

Children's Privacy

Lane Survivor does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The game does not require any personal information to play.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
