Lane Survivor

Neon Arcade Survival

A fast-paced arcade shooter with roguelike elements. Control your ship across three lanes, blast through waves of geometric enemies, collect upgrades, and survive as long as you can.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

Swipe or tap left/right to switch between the three lanes. Your ship fires automatically. Destroy enemies to collect gems and level up. Choose upgrades when you level up. Survive for 10 minutes to win!

What does Premium include?

Premium ($2.99) unlocks all 6 characters, unlimited daily runs, all ship skins, and enhanced visual effects. Free players can enjoy the full game with the Soldier character and 5 runs per day.

How do I restore purchases?

Open the game, tap the Settings button on the main menu, then tap "Restore Purchases". This will restore any previous Premium purchase linked to your Apple ID.

The game is crashing or lagging

Try these steps: 1) Close and restart the app. 2) Make sure your iOS is updated to the latest version. 3) Restart your device. If problems persist, please contact us with your device model and iOS version.

How do Daily Challenges work?

Each day features a unique challenge with special modifiers that change the gameplay. Everyone plays the same challenge. Free players get 1 attempt per day, Premium players get unlimited attempts.

What is Endless Mode?

Endless Mode is unlocked after you win your first standard run (survive 10 minutes). In Endless Mode, there's no time limit - the difficulty keeps scaling until you're overwhelmed. How long can you survive?
